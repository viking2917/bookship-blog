This Month's Tip: Finishing a book

If you are tired of seeing a Reading show up in your list of readings, or getting reading Reminders - for example if you are done with theÂ bookÂ - go to the Reading (theÂ bookÂ page), scroll to the top of the page, and hit "Done".
